Foosball Routine

Put the ball into play on your 5 bar.
Click the "Start training" button.
As soon as you hear "Go", start moving the ball on your 5 bar.
Follow the instructions!

Passes: "wall pass", "lane pass", "center pass"
Shots: "long left", "short left", "straight", "short right", "long right", "cut back left", "cut back right"
